Fizz Buzz is a programming exercise - it also originally is a group division word game.

players take turns counting up; replacing each number divisible by three (n%3=0) with "fizz", and five with "buzz" (n%5=0).

the numbers that are divisible by both 3 and 5 are replaced with "fizzbuzz".


this program is a model of fizz buzz - you can specify three numbers, and the words that they trigger.

you can also enter the default word, which is outputted when a number is not divisible by any of the provided numbers.
