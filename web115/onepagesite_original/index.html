FAQs

FAQs

Answers to common
questions about Najaf.ai

What is Najaf?

Najaf is an AI-powered, 3D-native product-creation platform that takes you from concept to commerce faster and at lower cost.

Who is Najaf for?

Brands, manufacturers, and e-commerce teams across apparel, accessories, footwear, jewelry, home goods, and other non-perishable consumer products.

Does Najaf replace my existing PLM or ERP?

Not today. Najaf streamlines design-through-merchandising and integrates with PLM/ERP systems via API so you keep downstream logistics intact.

Which file types does the 3D Configurator support?

GLB/GLTF 2.0 (preferred), FBX, OBJ, and STL. You can export 2D DXF, PNG/JPEG renders up to 8 K, and BOM/tech-pack PDFs.

Who owns the outputs my team creates?

You do. All renders, tech packs, and AI imagery generated in your workspace remain your intellectual property. Najaf only processes them to deliver the service.

How secure is my data?

Najaf is hosted on SOC 2 & ISO 27001-certified infrastructure (AWS + Cloudflare), uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.2+ in transit, and offers SSO, 2FA, and role-based access controls.
